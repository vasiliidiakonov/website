Challenge:
Graduating Graphic Design class of 2020 at British Higher School of Art and Design has been faced with an extraordinary challenge of graduating during the unprecedented circumstances of the pandemic. Instead of an in-person show, which has so far been the norm at the school, we decided to produce a website as a substitution. Read the whole story here: https://classof2020.online/info.html

Strategy:
Students organized themselves in teams. I was in one directly responsible for the design and interaction of the website-show. We wanted to introduce an idea of a website as a hybrid space, where activities usually reserved for school premises could be fully realized in a web format.

Solution:
The website is a vibrant microcosm of our graduating class, with projects occupying sections on the main page and events and info sections placed on the side buttons. “Button” style navigation has been chosen as the most engaging mode for a student exhibition, where engagement is matters most. Our work has been featured on It’s Nice That’s list of favorite degree show websites.
