Responsibilities:
Web-design and interaction, testing and user experience design for website and web-app. Motion graphics using Principle. Discovery sessions with the client.

Challenge:
Orientir studio were approached with an idea for a music quiz game that would be running off of the Spotify API. The project was in itâ€™s initial stages and the founders were desperately looking for direction. What could the whole thing look like? What does the identity convey?

Strategy:
I began working from scratch initially influenced by the Japanese dance arcades to create precedent and a similar sense of engagement. The working interface and the identity of the project would draw on the excitement that the arcade industry produced.

Solution:
The final solution is all about engagement, engaging attention, having the gull to make a bold decision. The quiz game was drawing on the original Spotify graphics and structure, as well as arcade fun to form a unique identity and imprint that would provide a way forward.
