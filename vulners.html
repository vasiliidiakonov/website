Vulners

Product design and strategy, branding, web

Challenge

Vulners needed a strategy to acquire and retain customers for their cybersecurity product suite. Their existing brand lacked cohesion across multiple products (Database, Scanner, Notifications), making it difficult to communicate their offering's strengths.

Solution

Developed a comprehensive design system and marketing strategy that unified the brand experience across all touchpoints—from product to marketing to customer acquisition.

Impact

Established consistent visual language across product ecosystem

Created scalable design system for marketing and product teams

Improved brand clarity and customer understanding

Research

Vulners offered a suite of cybersecurity products, but struggled to communicate how they worked together.

Defining Success Metrics

Within a rapidly growing suite of offerings, implementing new features required understanding customer goals and needs. We conducted research and detailed analysis to understand the next steps.

Key Insights:

• Customers needed to see the full ecosystem, not individual products

• Integration patterns were more important than features lists

• Trust signals (compliance, security standards) were critical

Design System

Created a comprehensive design system that worked across:

• Product UI (web applications, dashboards)

• Marketing (landing pages, campaigns)

• Data visualization (security reports, analytics)

Visual Language

Developed a unique illustration style that:

• Unified the Vulners ecosystem visually

• Made complex security concepts approachable

• Scaled across different contexts (abstract to specific)

• Maintained consistency from marketing to product

The illustration system could adapt from high-level concepts (data flows, integrations) to specific tools (Python SDK, Linux Scanner).

Implementation

Applied the design system across:

• Marketing site: Clear product positioning and customer journey

• Product pages: Feature-specific landing pages

• Documentation: Developer resources with consistent UI

• Customer portal: Unified dashboard experience

Results

Applied the design system across:

• Consistent brand experience across all touchpoints

• Faster development time with reusable components

• Improved customer understanding of product offering

Key Takeaways

  • Systems thinking: Designed for scalability across products and teams
  • Brand consistency: Unified visual language improved clarity
  • Accessibility: WCAG 2.1 standards ensured inclusive design
  • Cross-functional impact: System served marketing, product, and engineering
